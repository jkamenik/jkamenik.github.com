Intro

Cancan is my goto when I want complex permission behaviors.  It works very nicely with Device which is my goto for Authentication.  Below are some my tips for making things easier.

Pro-tip: Use Roles, apply them from most restricted to least restricted, and use separate methods/modules.

Permissions, especially on complex projects, can get ridiculously complex.  This is especially true when permissions are isolated based on attribute values.  If you have never had to deal with such complexity, you are either lucky or very near the start of the project.

By using roles you can make things like "a salesmen can manage lead that they create, but no other leads" easy to understand and also possible.
